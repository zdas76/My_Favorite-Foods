Vegetable Khichuri Recipe

This Instant Pot Moong Dal Khichdi with Vegetables

  1. It is a healthy, nutritious, one-pot meal
  2. It's easy.
  3. It gets done in under 30 minutes
  4. Packed with protein
  5. Perfect weeknight meal
  6. Easy to digest, which makes it a great meal for babies and the elderly
  7. Vegan, Vegetarian, and gluten-free


Masala Khichdi Ingredients

  1. White rice: I used sona masoori rice in this recipe. You may also use long-grain Basmati rice.
  2. Split green moong dal: I used split green moong dal with husk (skin) in this recipe. I did not soak the dal. Split yellow moong also can be used.
  3. Mixed vegetables: I used onions, carrots, green peas, green beans, cauliflower, and spinach.
  4. Cumin seeds: cumin seeds are a must in any Indian dal tempering, so do not skip them.
  5. Ginger: Use freshly minced ginger for the best flavor. You can also use store-bought ginger paste if that's what you have in hand.
  6. Green chilies: I have used 2 Thai green chilies. However, you can skip it if you want to reduce the heat.
  7. Ghee: Khichdi tastes best with homemade ghee. However, if you are a vegan, replace it with cooking oil.
  8. Dry Spices: I have used turmeric powder, red chili powder, and coriander powder. You can increase or decrease the spices according to your choice.
  9. Coriander leaves: for garnish.
